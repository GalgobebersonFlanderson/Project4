========================================================================
    Controls - Movement
========================================================================

- Use WASD or arrow keys to move the camera around the space
- Use LShift + W/Arrow UP to move forward, LShift + S/Arrow DOWN to move backwards
- Hold right mouse button and drag to rotate the camera
- Use Z(decrease) or X(increase) to change the camera FOV
- Use LSHIFT + R to reset FOV

========================================================================
    Controls - Lights
========================================================================
- Press "1" to toggle Directional Light
- Press "2" to toggle Point Light
- Press "3" to toggle Spot Light
- For all lights, use the keys TFGH to move the light around in space, as well as the keys U and J to either raise or lower the light
- For Spot Light, use the numberpad(if available) to rotate the light using keys 2486
